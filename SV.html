Introduction to Shimura varieties

Our aim in this course is to learn about Shimura varieties and the Langlands-Kottwitz method. The main topics are:

  • The definition of Shimura varieties in terms of hermitian locally symmetric domains
  • Their description by PEL moduli problems
  • Honda-Tate theory and point counting in characteristic p.

Lecture notes will be provided and updated regularly.
